Scene 2: Everyday Life Verbs

From morning to night, these verbs describe everything you do.

# Word Pronunciation In the Story… My Language
1 Eat /EET/ Eat a healthy breakfast every morning.
2 Drink /DRINK/ Drink eight glasses of water a day.
3 Cook /KOOK/ I cook dinner for my family every night.
4 Clean /KLEEN/ Clean your room before you go out.
5 Wash /WOSH/ Wash your hands before eating.
6 Open /OH-pen/ Open the window to let fresh air in.
7 Close /KLOHZ/ Close the door behind you.
8 Turn on /TURN ON/ Turn on the light, please.
9 Turn off /TURN OFF/ Turn off the TV before bed.
10 Buy /BY/ Buy milk at the grocery store.
11 Pay /PAY/ Pay for the groceries at the register.
12 Give /GIV/ Give the book to your friend.
13 Take /TAYK/ Take your medicine twice a day.
14 Put /PUT/ Put the dishes in the sink.
15 Get /GET/ Get ready for school by seven thirty.
16 Make /MAYK/ Make your bed every morning.
17 Read /REED/ Read a book to your child before bed.
18 Write /RYT/ Write your name at the top of the page.
19 Listen /LIS-en/ Listen carefully to the instructions.
20 Speak /SPEEK/ Speak slowly so I can understand.
21 Ask /ASK/ Ask the teacher if you have a question.
22 Answer /AN-ser/ Answer the phone when it rings.
23 Help /HELP/ Can you help me carry this box?
24 Try /TRY/ Try your best on every test.
25 Learn /LURN/ I want to learn English fluently.
